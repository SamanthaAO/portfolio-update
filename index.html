About Me

Samantha Orcutt

I am a Full Stack Web Developer that graduated cum laude in Environmental Studies from UW and has experience working in the financial and legal sectors. I am known for my friendly demeanor and hardworking, detail-oriented, work ethic. My background gives me a unique perspective on how to tackle problems and provides me with a very structured approach to get any task accomplished in an organized and concise fashion. I have a passion for efficiency and have a track record for process improvement. I was drawn to web development to enable me to implement further efficiencies both in how users interact with sites and how sites interact with data. I recently completed UW’s coding bootcamp where I developed a number of projects that I am very proud of. I am incredibly excited to apply my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and continue to grow in this expansive field.

In my free time (when I am not sitting at my desk coding away) I enjoy taking in all the natural splendor that the Pacific Northwest has to offer by going on hikes with my dog, Phoebe, and playing boardgames with friends.

For more information about me please check out my resume.
